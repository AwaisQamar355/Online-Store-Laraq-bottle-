Is the LARQ Bottle Swig Top dishwasher safe?
You can put the LARQ Bottle in the dishwasher (the cap should go on the top rack). However, we recommend hand washing it with warm water and soap for optimal product care.
Can I use it for hot or cold water?
LARQ Bottle Swig Top is for use with cold or room temperature water only. It can keep water cold for up to 24 hours. We’d suggest the LARQ Bottle Twist Top for both hot or cold features.
How does the LARQ Filter Straw work?
The LARQ Filter Straw is a portable water filtration system that combines a high performance Nano Zero filter technology with a seamless, low-pressure drinking experience. As you sip through the straw, our Nano Zero filter removes a range of contaminants like chlorine, lead, heavy metals, mercury, cadmium, particulates and more. (Filters are tested to NSF / ANSI 42 and 53 standards by 3rd party labs). 1 LARQ Bottle Filter replaces 300 single-use water bottles!
