Antimicrobial efficacy of the LARQ Bottle PureVis™ 500ml/17oz against E. coli
The purpose of this study was to determine how effective a LARQ Bottle was at deactivating E. coli at 1-min, 2-min, and 3-min treatments. The LARQ Bottle yielded a log reduction of 3.72 and deactivated 99.9775% of E.coli at 1 minute. The LARQ Bottle yielded a log reduction of 5.66 and deactivated 99.9998% of E. coli at 2 minutes. The LARQ Bottle yielded a log reduction of 6.08 and deactivated 99.9999% of E. coli at 3 minutes.
Antimicrobial efficacy of the LARQ Bottle PureVis™ 500ml/17oz against Salmonella
The purpose of this study was to determine how effective a LARQ Bottle was at deactivating Salmonella at 1-min, 2-min, and 3-min treatments. The LARQ Bottle yielded a log reduction of 1.81 and deactivated 98.1976% of Salmonella at 1 minute. The LARQ Bottle yielded a log reduction of 3.62 and deactivated 99.9733% of Salmonella at 2 minutes. The LARQ Bottle yielded a log reduction of 6.62 and deactivated 99.9999% of Salmonella at 3 minutes.
Antimicrobial efficacy of various LARQ Bottles against E.coli
The purpose of this study was to measure the effectiveness of various LARQ Bottles at deactivating E.coli at different times. The report shows that all tested LARQ Bottles achieved at least a 99.8% reduction of E.coli at 20 seconds.
Antibacterial efficacy of LARQ UV-C Pitcher against aqueous heterotrophic bacteria
The purpose of this procedure was to test the effectiveness of the UVC function in LARQs pitcher at inhibiting the growth of, and deactivation of aqueous heterotrophic bacteria in water samples.
